# Accrued Liabilities

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Article04.pdf

## Definition

Obligations that have been incurred for goods or services received but have not yet been paid for as of the reporting date.

## Key Points

- Recognizes the expense in the income statement during the period the service was provided.
- Increases liabilities on the balance sheet until the cash payment is made.
- Payment in a later period reduces both cash and the liability without impacting the income statement of that later period.
