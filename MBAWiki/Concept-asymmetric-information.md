# Asymmetric Information

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 7 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A condition where one party in a transaction possesses more or better information than the other party.

## Key Points

- Leads to market failures
- Often categorized as hidden information or hidden action
- Can be mitigated through signaling or research

(No formulas)

## Examples

- Mechanic knowing more about a car than the owner
- Doctor knowing more about medical necessity than the patient
