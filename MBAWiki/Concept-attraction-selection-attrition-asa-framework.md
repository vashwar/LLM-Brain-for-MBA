# Attraction-Selection-Attrition (ASA) Framework

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

A psychological model explaining how organizational culture persists by attracting similar types of people, selecting those who fit, and losing those who do not.

## Key Points

- Attraction: People are drawn to organizations with values similar to their own.
- Selection: Organizations hire individuals who fit their existing culture.
- Selective Attrition: Employees who do not fit the culture are more likely to leave or be pushed out.
