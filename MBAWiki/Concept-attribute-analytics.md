# Attribute Analytics

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

The traditional form of people analytics that focuses on individual characteristics categorized as either traits or states.

## Key Points

- Trait data: Facts that don't change (e.g., ethnicity, work history).
- State data: Facts that do change (e.g., age, tenure, bonuses, education level).
- Often insufficient on its own to predict complex workplace performance because it ignores interactions.

- Diversity metrics based on gender and ethnic makeup.
