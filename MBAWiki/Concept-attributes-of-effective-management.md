# Attributes of Effective Management

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_2_WE.pdf, LP_2024_class_1_WE.pdf

## Definition

A set of research-identified behaviors and characteristics that correlate with high performance and team success. This includes the implementation of structured management practices, which is empirically shown to be strongly correlated with increased firm productivity, innovation, and successful delivery of services.

## Key Points

- **Behaviors of Effective Managers (e.g., Google's Project Oxygen):**
    - Being a good coach.
    - Empowering the team and avoiding micromanagement.
    - Showing interest in team members' success and well-being.
    - Being productive and results-oriented.
    - Possessing key technical skills to advise the team.
    - Communicating a clear vision and strategy.
- **Impact of Structured Management:**
    - The World Management Survey (Bloom, Sadun, and Van Reenen) demonstrates that many organizations are poorly managed worldwide.
    - Better management is positively correlated with higher productivity and higher levels of innovation.
    - Improving management practices is considered a "radical" way to increase global incomes and service delivery.
