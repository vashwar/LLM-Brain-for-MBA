# Auctions

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Fall 2024 - EWMBA 201A-LEC-31A _ 32A - Economics for Business Decision Making on 8_24_2024 (Sat)_Captions_English (United States).txt

## Definition

A mechanism for selling goods where bidders compete to purchase an item, often used to reveal true willingness to pay.

## Key Points

- English auctions allow public ascending bids
- Sealed-bid auctions keep bids private
- Revenue Equivalence Theorem suggests various formats may yield similar expected revenue under certain assumptions
- Reserve prices can increase revenue but risk no sale
