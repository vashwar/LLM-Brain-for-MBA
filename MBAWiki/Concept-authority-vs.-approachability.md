# Authority vs. Approachability

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** TheAuthenticityParadox (1).pdf

## Definition

The tension leaders face between demonstrating expertise and maintaining professional distance (authority) versus building relationships through empathy and warmth (approachability).

## Key Points

- To be authoritative, a leader privileges their own knowledge and experience.
- To be approachable, a leader emphasizes team input and empathy.
- Transitions to larger roles often require a shift toward authority, which can create an authenticity crisis for those who prefer approachability.
