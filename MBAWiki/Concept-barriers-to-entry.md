# Barriers to Entry

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 5 Weekend notes.pdf

## Definition

Obstacles that make it difficult or impossible for new firms to enter a market, protecting incumbent firms from competition and allowing for sustainable profits.

## Key Points

- Patents
- Trade secrets
- Network externalities
- Economies of scale
- Control of resources
- Brand recognition
- Lobbying
- Switching costs
