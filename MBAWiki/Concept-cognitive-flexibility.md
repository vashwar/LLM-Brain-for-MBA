# Cognitive Flexibility

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** PersuadingTheUnpersuadable.pdf

## Definition

The mental ability to switch between thinking about two different concepts, and to think about multiple concepts simultaneously to rethink convictions and adapt views.

## Key Points

- Success in turbulent environments depends on the wisdom to question one's own convictions.
- Leaders must surround themselves with people who have the courage to challenge their worst instincts.
- It involves moving away from the 'strength of convictions' toward an openness to being wrong.
