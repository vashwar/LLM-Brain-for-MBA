# Conditions for Collective Intelligence

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_6_WE-1.pdf

## Definition

The specific environmental and structural requirements necessary for a group to perform more effectively than its best individual member.

## Key Points

- Diversity: Leveraging different backgrounds and perspectives to cover information gaps.
- Independence: Ensuring members' opinions are not influenced by the stated opinions of others.
- Decentralization: Distributing power so that local or specialized knowledge can be surfaced.
