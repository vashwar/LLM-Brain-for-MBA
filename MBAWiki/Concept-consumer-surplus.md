# Consumer Surplus

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition

The economic measure of the happiness or benefit consumers receive from a product, calculated as the difference between their maximum willingness to pay and the actual price paid.

## Key Points

- Calculated as the area below the demand curve and above the price line.
- Represents 'leftover' value after a purchase.
- Increases as the purchase price decreases.

## Formulas & Equations

- Consumer Surplus = Total Value - Total Expenditure

## Examples

- Calculating value for pizza slices within the Perez-Nunez family.
