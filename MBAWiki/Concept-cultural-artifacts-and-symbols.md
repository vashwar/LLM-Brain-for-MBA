# Cultural Artifacts and Symbols

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

Visible elements of an organization, including physical objects, language, and events, that represent and reinforce underlying cultural values.

## Key Points

- Mission Statements: Mottos linking company objectives to individual motivation.
- Ceremonies: Events that celebrate achievements and foster cohesion.
- Special Physical Settings: Architecture or decor that reinforces cultural identity.
- Stories and Scripts: Narratives that turn ordinary actions into heroic role models.
- Jargon/Language: Specialized vocabulary that identifies insiders and communicates assumptions.
- Costumes/Uniforms: Clothing that reinforces collective identity.
