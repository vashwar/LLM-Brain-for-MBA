# Culture as a Control Mechanism

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

The use of shared organizational values and norms to guide employee behavior as an alternative to formal rules and supervision.

## Key Points

- Provides simple decision rules that are more flexible than long manuals.
- Creates habits and codes of conduct that influence private behavior.
- Facilitates cooperation and reduces internal transaction costs.

## Examples

- Employees making decisions based on 'Customer Obsession' at Amazon without consulting a handbook.
- A strong culture that keeps people productive even when they are not being watched.
