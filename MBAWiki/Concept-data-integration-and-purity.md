# Data Integration and Purity

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** TheyBoughtInNowTheyWantToBailOutHBRCaseStudy (1).pdf

## Definition

The technical and organizational challenge of ensuring that information collected at decentralized points is accurate, consistent, and usable for centralized strategic analysis.

## Key Points

- Information collected at the store level (POS) is often perceived as 'unreliable' or 'dirty' by analysts.
- Effective CRM depends on the 'purity' of the data—ensuring marketing databases and transaction databases are fully integrated.
- Data integration is frequently the point where large-scale technology systems 'break down' in practice.
