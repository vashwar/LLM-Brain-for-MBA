# Decision Trees

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_4_WE-1.pdf

## Definition

A visual and analytical tool used to map out the chain of events, including choices and chance outcomes, to calculate the expected value of different branches.

## Key Points

- Outcome lines represent the result of events.
- Chance nodes (circles) represent probabilistic events.
- Choice nodes (squares) represent points where a decision is made.
- Values and probabilities are computed to find the best path.
