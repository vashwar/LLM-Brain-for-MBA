# Digital Exhaust

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

The data trail created by employees' everyday digital activities, including emails, chats, file transfers, and collaboration platform logs.

## Key Points

- Serves as the raw material for relational analytics.
- Passive collection is less biased and more accurate than self-reported surveys.
- Provides real-time, dynamic views of organizational networks.
- Requires clear transparency and privacy policies regarding employee tracking.
