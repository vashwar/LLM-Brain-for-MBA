# Distancing Traits

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** CouldYourPersonalityDerailYourCareer.pdf

## Definition

A cluster of dark-side traits that act as obvious turnoffs to others, pushing people away and hindering trust.

## Key Points

- Includes traits such as Excitable, Skeptical, Cautious, Reserved, and Leisurely.
- These traits have a consistently negative impact on work attitudes, leadership effectiveness, and interpersonal skills.
- Often result in poor 360-degree reviews and performance ratings.

## Examples

- Leisurely: Appearing polite and cooperative on the surface while privately resisting cooperation or engaging in passive-aggressive behavior.
- Skeptical: Being so distrustful and cynical that it becomes impossible to build meaningful work relationships.
