# Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_8_WE.pdf

## Definition

A conceptual framework and organizational practice aimed at ensuring that individuals from all backgrounds are represented, treated fairly, and integrated into the workplace and market.

## Key Points

- Organizations serve as drivers of both equality and inequality through their internal and external practices.
- Impacts employees through hiring, compensation, and retention.
- Impacts customers through service quality, pricing, and innovation.
- Effective DEI management requires moving beyond simple hiring to fostering an inclusive culture.
