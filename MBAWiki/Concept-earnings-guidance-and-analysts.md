# Earnings Guidance and Analysts

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Spring 2025 - EWMBA 202-LEC-31A _ 32A - Financial Accounting (Spring 2025)_Captions_English (United States).txt

## Definition

The process where companies provide projections for future performance, which analysts then use to set market expectations.

## Key Points

- Quarterly reporting is a major ritual for public companies.
- Stock price movement is often dictated by 'beating' analyst expectations (the consensus forecast).
- Guidance is not legally required but is common practice.
- Critics like Warren Buffett and Jamie Dimon argue that quarterly guidance fosters 'short-termism'.
