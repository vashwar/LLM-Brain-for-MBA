# Economies of Scope

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Fall 2024 - EWMBA 201A-LEC-31A _ 32A - Economics for Business Decision Making_Captions_English (United States).txt

## Definition

Cost advantages that a firm obtains when it produces a variety of products using shared resources.

## Key Points

- Allows firms to share fixed costs like R&D, distribution, and infrastructure across multiple product lines.
- Examples include Amazon leveraging warehouse/shipping infrastructure for books to sell other items.

## Examples

- Amazon's evolution from books to a general retailer.
- Apple selling iPhones, iPads, and Mac computers using shared design and technical R&D.
