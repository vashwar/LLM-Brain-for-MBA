# Efficiency Signature

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

A team-level network configuration defined by high internal density and high external range, optimized for completing projects on time.

## Key Points

- Internal density refers to the amount of interaction and interconnectedness within the team, which builds trust.
- External range refers to the team's ability to reach diverse experts outside the team to source information.
- This signature is highly predictive of project speed and resource acquisition.
