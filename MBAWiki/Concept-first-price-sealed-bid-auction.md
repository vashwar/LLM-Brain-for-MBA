# First-Price Sealed-Bid Auction

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 3 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

An auction where bidders submit secret bids and the highest bidder wins, paying the amount they bid.

## Key Points

- Bidders must 'shade' their bids (bid below their valuation) to capture surplus.
- Winning probability decreases as the bid increases.
- Optimal bid depends on the number of other bidders.

## Formulas & Equations

- Optimal Bid = (L/N) + (Valuation * (N-1)/N)

## Examples

- Real estate sales
