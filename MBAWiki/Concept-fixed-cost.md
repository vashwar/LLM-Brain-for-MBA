# Fixed Cost

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 4 Weekend-1.pdf

## Definition

Costs that do not change regardless of the quantity of output produced.

## Key Points

- Remains constant even if production is zero
- Includes overheads, R&D, and long-term lease agreements
- Distinction from variable costs is essential for cost analysis

## Formulas & Equations

- TC = FC + VC

## Examples

- Research and Development (R&D) at Samsung

## Notes

Fixed costs must be spread thinner as quantity increases.
