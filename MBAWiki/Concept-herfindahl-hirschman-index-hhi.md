# Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI)

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Fall 2024 - EWMBA 201A-LEC-31A _ 32A - Economics for Business Decision Making_Captions_English (United States) (2).txt

## Definition

A measure of market concentration calculated by squaring the market share of each firm in an industry and summing the resulting numbers.

## Key Points

- Ranges from 0 (perfect competition) to 1 (monopoly).
- Used by antitrust authorities to evaluate the competitive impact of mergers.
- Definition of the 'relevant market' significantly impacts the calculated HHI.

## Formulas & Equations

- HHI = Σ(market_share_i)^2

## Examples

- Google search advertising
- Netflix streaming market
