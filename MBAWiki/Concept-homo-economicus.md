# Homo Economicus

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A theoretical model of human behavior assuming individuals are 100% selfish, 100% rational, and 100% informed. This model serves as the organizing principle for much of economic analysis.

## Key Points

- 100% Selfish: Ignores effects on others.
- 100% Rational: Makes optimal decisions instantaneously.
- 100% Informed: Possesses all relevant data.
- Simplifies complex human behavior to allow for quick decision-making.
- Real-world humans often deviate from these assumptions (altruism, cognitive limits, information gaps).
