# Identity as an Evolving Narrative

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** TheAuthenticityParadox (1).pdf

## Definition

The view that professional identity is a life story resulting from a person's selective appropriation of past, present, and future experiences.

## Key Points

- Identity is not fixed; it is an edited story that must change as we grow.
- Old stories (personal narratives) can become outdated and act as barriers to advancement.
- Effective leaders periodically revise their stories to resonate with their current role and future aspirations.
