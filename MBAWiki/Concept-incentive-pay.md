# Incentive Pay

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 7 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

Compensation structures designed to align the agent's incentives with the principal's goals by tying pay to specific performance metrics.

## Key Points

- Can backfire if metrics are misaligned
- Risk of multitasking bias
- Often used to motivate effort

## Examples

- Sales commissions
- Stock options for CEOs
- Bonuses based on standardized test scores
