# Income Elasticity of Demand

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 2 with notes.pdf

## Definition

A measure of how sensitive the demand for a good is to changes in the income of consumers.

## Key Points

- Calculated as % change in quantity demanded / % change in income
- Used to classify goods as normal or inferior

## Formulas & Equations

- Income Elasticity = (% change in Quantity) / (% change in Income)

## Examples

- Tesla Model 3 as a luxury good (elasticity of 2)
- Ramen noodles as an inferior good
