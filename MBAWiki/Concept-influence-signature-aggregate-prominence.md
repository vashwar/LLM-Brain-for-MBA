# Influence Signature (Aggregate Prominence)

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

A measure of an individual's influence based on the strength of their connections and how well-connected those contacts are to others.

## Key Points

- Calculated by looking at the prominence of a person's connections (connectivity of connections).
- The most influential people are not necessarily the most popular but the most 'prominent' in the network structure.
- Prominent influencers can spread ideas further and change others' behavior more effectively than senior leadership alone.

## Examples

- A medical device manufacturer using influencers with high aggregate prominence to drive adoption of new compliance policies.
- Search engines using similar logic (like PageRank) to rank the importance of websites.
