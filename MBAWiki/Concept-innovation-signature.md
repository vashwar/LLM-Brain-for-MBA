# Innovation Signature

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

A team-level network configuration defined by low internal density and high external range, optimized for creative breakthroughs.

## Key Points

- Low internal density ensures diverse perspectives and healthy debate (creative friction) rather than groupthink.
- High external range allows the team to source ideas and secure buy-in from external champions.
- Unlike efficiency teams, innovation teams benefit from some level of internal discord.

## Examples

- An automobile company's product development team that debated fiercely but had wide global connections, leading to twice the process innovations of other teams.
