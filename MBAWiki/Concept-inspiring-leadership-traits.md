# Inspiring Leadership Traits

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_1_WE.pdf

## Definition

A set of behaviors and characteristics found in effective leaders that align employee behavior with organizational goals during crises or routine operations.

## Key Points

- Calmness and confidence under pressure.
- A focus on others (altruism), such as ensuring the safety of subordinates before oneself.
- Visionary decision-making, which involves envisioning multiple solutions to a single problem.

## Examples

- Captain Chesley 'Sully' Sullenberger remaining calm and being the last person off the plane during the 'Miracle on the Hudson'.
- Contrasted with Captain Schettino (Costa Concordia), who abandoned ship and failed to anticipate route problems.
