# Inverse Demand Function

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 2 with notes.pdf

## Definition

A function expressing the price of a product as a function of the quantity demanded.

## Key Points

- Conventionally uses P on the Y-axis and Q on the X-axis
- Attributed to Alfred Marshall
- Essential for deriving total and marginal revenue

## Formulas & Equations

- P = f(Q)

## Examples

- P = 400 - 0.25Q
