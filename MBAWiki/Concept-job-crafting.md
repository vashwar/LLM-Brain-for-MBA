# Job Crafting

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_5_WE.pdf

## Definition

A technique to promote intrinsic motivation by allowing employees to proactively redefine and reshape the boundaries of their work.

## Key Points

- Task Crafting: Altering the number, type, or nature of the tasks performed.
- Relational Crafting: Altering the frequency, intensity, and style of interactions with others at work.
- Cognitive Crafting: Altering how one perceives the tasks and the overall meaning of their work.
