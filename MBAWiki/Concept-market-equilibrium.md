# Market Equilibrium

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition

The state where the quantity supplied by firms exactly equals the quantity demanded by consumers, resulting in an equilibrium price and quantity.

## Key Points

- Excess supply occurs when price is above equilibrium.
- Excess demand occurs when price is below equilibrium.
- The equilibrium is the point where the supply and demand curves intersect.

## Formulas & Equations

- Q_s = Q_d

## Examples

- Price adjustments to clear market shortages or surpluses.
