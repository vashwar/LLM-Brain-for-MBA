# Market Power

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 5 Weekend notes.pdf

## Definition

The ability of a firm (or buyer) to influence the market price of a good or service. Firms with market power can increase prices to boost revenue and profit, while buyers with market power can decrease prices to reduce costs.

## Key Points

- Exists on a spectrum
- Determined by firm behavior rather than just the number of firms
- Can be exercised by both sellers and buyers
