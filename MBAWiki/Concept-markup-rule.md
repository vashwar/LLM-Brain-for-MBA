# Markup Rule

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 2 with notes.pdf

## Definition

A formula used to determine the optimal price for a product based on marginal cost and demand elasticity to maximize profits.

## Key Points

- Also known as Ramsey pricing
- Optimal price is inversely related to the absolute value of price elasticity
- Requires MR = MC

## Formulas & Equations

- (P - MC) / P = -1 / Ed

## Examples

- Setting price at $20 for a product with $10 MC and -2 elasticity
