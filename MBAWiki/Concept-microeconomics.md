# Microeconomics

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition

The branch of economics that focuses on the individual decisions of firms and individuals regarding the allocation of resources and the pricing of goods. It examines how these individual choices interact within specific markets.

## Key Points

- Analyzes choices at the granular level.
- Analyzes individual decision-making.
- Distinguished from macroeconomics, which deals with aggregate variables or aggregated choices.
- Provides the foundation for strategy, marketing, and human resources.
- Focuses on topics like pricing and firm behavior.
