# Mobilization via Shared Purpose

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_10_WE.pdf

## Definition

The use of rhetorical and communication strategies to align a group and motivate them toward collective action.

## Key Points

- Connect to cultural icons or shared history to provide context.
- Vividly describe the present reality to create a sense of urgency or need.
- Express an optimistic vision of the future to inspire hope.
- Make the message personal to the audience without being egocentric.
- Create and repeat a unifying phrase to build cohesion.
