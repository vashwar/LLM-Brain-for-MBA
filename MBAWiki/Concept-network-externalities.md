# Network Externalities

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 5 Weekend notes.pdf

## Definition

A phenomenon where the value of a product or service increases as more people use it, creating a barrier to entry for new competitors.

## Key Points

- New entrants struggle to reach critical mass
- Makes it difficult for new platforms to compete with incumbents
- Can lead to 'winner-take-all' market outcomes
