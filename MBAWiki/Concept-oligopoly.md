# Oligopoly

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 6 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A market structure where a small number of firms control the majority of the market share, leading to strategic interdependence.

## Key Points

- Decisions are interdependent; one firm's action impacts others.
- Barriers to entry exist.
- Can involve competition in quantity or price.
