# Operating Margin

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Session 01.pdf

## Definition

A profitability ratio that measures the percentage of revenue remaining after accounting for operating expenses.

## Key Points

- Used to compare operational efficiency across different firms.
- Excludes non-operating items like interest and taxes.
- Provides insight into core business performance.

## Formulas & Equations

- Operating Margin = Operating Profit / Sales

## Examples

- Calculating Netflix's operating margin as Operating Profit divided by Sales.
