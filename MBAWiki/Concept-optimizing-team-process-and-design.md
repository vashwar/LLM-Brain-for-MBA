# Optimizing Team Process and Design

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_6_WE-1.pdf

## Definition

Structured interventions and group designs used to improve decision-making and leverage the full potential of a diverse team.

## Key Points

- Individual Pre-work: Members should work individually before meeting as a group to minimize social influence.
- Appointing a Contrarian: Assigning a 'devil's advocate' to question evidence and existing consensus.
- Equal Participation: Sourcing ideas from the most junior person first to prevent senior leaders from biasing the discussion.
- Idea Sourcing: Using facilitators and blind submissions to source ideas without immediate evaluation.
