# Ordinary Least Squares

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 2 with notes.pdf

## Definition

A statistical method used to estimate the parameters of a linear regression model by minimizing the sum of squared errors between observed and predicted values.

## Key Points

- Finds the best-fitting line
- Dependent variable is quantity, independent is price
- Used for empirical demand estimation

## Formulas & Equations

- Minimize Σ(Actual Q - Predicted Q)^2

## Examples

- Estimating airline ticket demand
