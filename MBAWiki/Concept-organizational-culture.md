# Organizational Culture

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

The pattern of beliefs, values, symbols, and behavioral expectations (norms) shared by an organization’s members, often described as 'how things are done around here.'

## Key Points

- Acts as a lens through which members see the world.
- Involves orientation and 'learning the ropes' for new employees.
- Created and maintained through attraction, selection, socialization, and selective attrition.
