# Organizational Socialization

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

The process through which new employees learn the norms, structure, policies, and values of an organization to build consensus.

## Key Points

- Often involves orientation programs and buddy systems.
- Aims to align new hire behaviors with company expectations.
- Reduces uncertainty by teaching 'the ropes' of the organization.
