# Passive financial investments

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Session 07.pdf

## Definition

Investments where the investor owns less than 20% of the investee’s voting stock, treated as non-operating financial assets.

## Key Points

- Marked-to-market at each reporting date.
- Unrealized gains or losses are recognized in the income statement.
- Considered passive rather than strategic.

## Formulas & Equations

- Ending Value = Fair Market Value at Reporting Date

## Examples

- Passive stock portfolios
- Berkshire Hathaway's minor stock holdings

## Notes

Changes in market price create volatility in the income statement.
