# Post-Settlement Settlements (PSS)

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** Chapter10MakingRationalDecisionsInNegotiations.pdf

## Definition

A process where parties, after reaching an initial agreement, look for further improvements that could make both sides better off.

## Key Points

- The initial agreement serves as the new BATNA for both parties.
- Parties can revert to the original deal if no improvement is found.
- Aims for a Pareto-superior agreement (efficiency).

## Examples

- After signing a contract, two partners spend ten minutes seeing if a different work allocation would save them both time.
