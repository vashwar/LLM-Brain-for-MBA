# Predictors of Job Performance

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_7_WE.pdf

## Definition

Various metrics and assessment tools evaluated based on their statistical ability to forecast how well a candidate will perform in a role.

## Key Points

- General Mental Ability (GMA) tests are among the strongest predictors of performance.
- Work sample tests and structured interviews have high predictive validity.
- Years of education and unstructured interviews are relatively weak predictors of actual job performance.
- Combining multiple predictors (e.g., GMA + integrity tests) increases the accuracy of the hiring decision.
