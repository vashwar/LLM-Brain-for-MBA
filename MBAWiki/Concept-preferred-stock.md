# Preferred Stock

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Spring 2025 - EWMBA 202-LEC-31A _ 32A - Financial Accounting (Spring 2025)_Captions_English (United States) (4).txt

## Definition

A class of stock that provides specific preferences over common stock, usually regarding dividend payments and liquidation priorities.

## Key Points

- Holders get paid before common shareholders in liquidation events
- Often used by VCs to protect their investment downside
- Can be 'convertible' into common stock at the holder's discretion

## Formulas & Equations

- Liquidation preference = Investment Amount * Multiple

## Examples

- 1x Nonparticipating convertible preferred shares in startup funding
