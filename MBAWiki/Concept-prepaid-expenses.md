# Prepaid expenses

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Article04.pdf

## Definition

An asset account representing payments made for goods or services that will be consumed or used in future accounting periods.

## Key Points

- Cash decreases while the Prepaid Expense asset increases initially.
- The asset is gradually reduced as the benefit is consumed, with the corresponding amount recorded as an expense.
- Ensures costs are matched to the period in which they contribute to generating revenue.
