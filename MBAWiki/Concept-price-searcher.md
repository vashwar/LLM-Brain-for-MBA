# Price-searcher

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 5 Weekend notes.pdf

## Definition

A firm that faces a downward-sloping demand curve, meaning it has the ability to influence the market price by changing its quantity supplied. Changes in output result in price adjustments.

## Key Points

- Increasing quantity leads to a price drop
- Marginal revenue is affected by both the gain from selling more units and the loss from lower prices on existing units
