# Price-taker

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 5 Weekend notes.pdf

## Definition

A firm in a perfectly competitive market that has no influence over the market price and must accept the price determined by supply and demand. The firm's demand curve is perfectly elastic (horizontal).

## Key Points

- Output is insignificant relative to total market supply
- Marginal Revenue equals Market Price
- Individual firm demand is a horizontal line

## Formulas & Equations

- MR = P

## Examples

- Individual corn farmers in the US
