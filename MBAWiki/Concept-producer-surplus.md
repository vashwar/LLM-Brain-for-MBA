# Producer Surplus

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition

The benefit producers receive from selling a product at a market price that is higher than the minimum price they would have been willing to accept.

## Key Points

- Often visualized as the area above the supply curve and below the market price.
- Contrasts with Consumer Surplus.

(No formulas)

## Examples

- Difference between the minimum wage a barista accepts and the market rate.
