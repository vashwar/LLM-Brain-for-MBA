# Reaction Function

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 6 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A mathematical relationship that describes a firm's optimal output choice as a function of its competitor's output choice.

## Key Points

- Derived from MR=MC condition.
- Intersection of reaction functions defines the Cournot equilibrium.
- Reflects symmetric or asymmetric firm strategies.

## Formulas & Equations

- QA = f(QB)

## Examples

- QA = 45 - 0.5QB
