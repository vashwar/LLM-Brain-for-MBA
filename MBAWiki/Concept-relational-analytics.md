# Relational Analytics

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

The science of human social networks applied to organizational performance, focusing on the interplay and communication patterns among people rather than individual attributes alone.

## Key Points

- Focuses on who people know rather than just who they are.
- Identifies 'structural signatures'—patterns in data that correlate with performance outcomes.
- Uses data from digital transactions (digital exhaust) rather than traditional surveys.
- Complements traditional people analytics which relies on individual attribute data.
