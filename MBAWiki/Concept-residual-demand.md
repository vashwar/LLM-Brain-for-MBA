# Residual Demand

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 6 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

The demand curve facing a firm after accounting for the quantity supplied by its competitors.

## Key Points

- Calculated by shifting market demand left by the competitor's quantity.
- Used to derive the marginal revenue curve for individual firms in oligopoly.
- Determines the optimal quantity for the firm.

## Formulas & Equations

- P = Market Demand - Q_competitor

## Examples

- Airbus demand after Boeing produces 50 units
