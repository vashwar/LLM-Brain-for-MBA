# Second-Price Auction

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 3 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

An auction format (also known as a Vickrey Auction) where the winner is the person who submits the highest bid, but they pay the price of the second-highest bid.

## Key Points

- Dominant strategy is to bid your true valuation.
- Provides an incentive for truthful bidding.
- Previously used by Google for ad slots.

## Formulas & Equations

- Winner Pays = V(2nd highest)

## Examples

- Vickrey Auction
- Historical Google AdSense slots

## Notes

Noted for its simplicity in strategy compared to other auction formats.
