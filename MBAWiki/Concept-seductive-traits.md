# Seductive Traits

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** CouldYourPersonalityDerailYourCareer.pdf

## Definition

A cluster of dark-side traits that pull people in and are often associated with assertive, charismatic leadership styles.

## Key Points

- Includes traits such as Bold, Mischievous, Colorful, and Imaginative.
- Can have short-term positive effects, such as helping a leader gain followers or drive growth in entrepreneurial settings.
- Long-term risks include overestimating one's worth, arrogance, and making impulsive, reckless decisions.

## Examples

- Bold: Leaders who are ultra-confident to the point of grandiosity, which can lead to ignoring critical feedback.
- Colorful: Leaders who are entertaining and expressive but may become socially obtuse and attention-seeking.
