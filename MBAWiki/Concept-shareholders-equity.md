# Shareholders' Equity

**Course:** Financial Accounting
**Source:** Article01.pdf

## Definition

The interest of the owners in the company, calculated as total assets minus total liabilities.

## Key Points

- Comprised of contributed capital and retained earnings
- Contributed capital is the total cash raised by selling shares
- Retained earnings represent cumulative net income minus dividends paid

## Formulas & Equations

- Shareholders’ Equity = Assets - Liabilities
- End Period Retained Earnings = Beginning Retained Earnings + Net Income - Dividends

- Applied Materials total shareholders' equity on 10/29/2017
