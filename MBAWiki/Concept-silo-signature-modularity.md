# Silo Signature (Modularity)

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** BetterPeopleAnalyticsMeasureWhoTheyKnowNotJustWhoTheyAre.pdf

## Definition

An organizational state where groups or departments are heavily isolated, communicating mostly within themselves rather than with other units.

## Key Points

- Measured by modularity: the ratio of internal communication to external communication.
- A ratio greater than 5:1 is considered detrimentally siloed.
- Silos prevent the sharing of valuable data and insights across the organization.

## Formulas & Equations

- Internal-to-External Communication Ratio > 5:1

## Examples

- An advocacy group where the operations department had a 13:1 internal-to-external communication ratio and failed to share website traffic data with marketing.
