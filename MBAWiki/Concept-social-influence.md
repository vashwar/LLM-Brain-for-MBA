# Social Influence

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_11_WE.pdf

## Definition

The process by which a person's beliefs or behaviors are modified by their social connections.

## Key Points

- Connected individuals become more similar over time due to interaction.
- Social influence amplifies differences in outcomes, such as popularity or adoption rates.
- It makes outcomes (like market success) less predictable.
