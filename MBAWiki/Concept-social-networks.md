# Social Networks

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_11_WE.pdf

## Definition

Invisible but measurable systems of connections between individuals that act as conduits for resources, ideas, and professional opportunities.

## Key Points

- Networks provide access or barriers to opportunities and resources.
- They allow or prevent the mobilization of others.
- They influence individual beliefs, support, and the adoption of behaviors.
