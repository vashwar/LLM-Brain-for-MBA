# Strong Culture

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

A culture that is intensely held, widely shared, and consistently displayed across an organization.

## Key Points

- Characterized by high levels of consistency in behavior.
- Requires consensus among members regarding values.
- Includes high intensity of belief and a unique or distinct identity.

(No formulas)

## Examples

- Consistent display of values across all levels of a hierarchy.
- Widely shared norms that reduce the need for formal manuals.
