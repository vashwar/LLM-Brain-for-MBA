# Switching Costs

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 5 Weekend notes.pdf

## Definition

The costs (monetary or non-monetary) that a customer incurs when switching from one brand or product to another.

## Key Points

- Acts as a barrier to entry
- Reduces consumer willingness to switch
- Companies often create them to lock in users
- Can include contract termination fees or time spent relearning software
