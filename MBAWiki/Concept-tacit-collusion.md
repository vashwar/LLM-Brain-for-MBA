# Tacit Collusion

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 7 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A situation where firms in an oligopoly implicitly coordinate their pricing or output strategies to maximize joint profits without explicit agreement.

## Key Points

- Often unstable
- Difficult to maintain without formal agreements
- Closely related to the Prisoner's Dilemma

(No formulas)

## Examples

- Firms maintaining high prices without formal meetings
