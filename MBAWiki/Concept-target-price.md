# Target Price

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_9_WE.pdf

## Definition

The ambitious but plausible outcome that a negotiator aspires to achieve.

## Key Points

- Also known as the aspiration point.
- Negotiators should focus on their target price during the negotiation to achieve better outcomes, but keep their reservation price in mind to avoid bad deals.

## Formulas & Equations

(No formulas)

## Examples

- A job seeker hoping for a $100,000 salary while their minimum acceptable (reservation) price is $93,000.
