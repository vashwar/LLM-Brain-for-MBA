# Teaching to the Test

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 7 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A phenomenon where teachers focus curriculum and instruction primarily on preparing students for standardized tests at the expense of broader educational goals.

## Key Points

- Example of incentive misalignment
- Undermines long-term learning objectives like grit and creativity
- Result of poorly designed bonus structures
