# Threats to Experimental Validity

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_2_WE.pdf

## Definition

Specific challenges and phenomena that can bias the results or limit the generalizability of organizational experiments.

## Key Points

- Hawthorne Effect: Subjects change behavior because they know they are being studied.
- Non-Compliance: Subjects fail to follow the treatment protocol.
- Contamination: Information or treatment spills over from the treatment group to the control group.
- Attrition: High dropout rates, especially if they differ between groups.
- Small Sample Size: Difficulty in detecting significant effects (lack of statistical power).
