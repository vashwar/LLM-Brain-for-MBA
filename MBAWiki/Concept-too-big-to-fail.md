# Too Big to Fail

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 7 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

The concept that certain financial institutions are so large and interconnected that their failure would cause a catastrophic economic collapse, leading the government to intervene.

## Key Points

- Creates moral hazard by encouraging excessive risk-taking
- Expectation of government bailout
- Distorts market incentives
