# Toxic Workers

**Course:** Leading People
**Source:** LP_2024_class_12_WE (1).pdf

## Definition

Employees who, despite often being highly productive, regularly engage in behaviors that hurt others or negatively impact the work environment.

## Key Points

- Toxic workers create negative externalities like lawsuits and negative peer effects.
- The cost of a toxic worker often outweighs the value of a 'superstar' employee (top 1%).
- Avoiding toxic hires can save a firm more money than hiring a high-performer.

## Examples
