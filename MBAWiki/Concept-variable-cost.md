# Variable Cost

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 4 Weekend-1.pdf

## Definition

Costs that fluctuate directly with the level of output produced.

## Key Points

- Dependent on quantity produced
- Includes raw materials, direct labor, and energy consumption
- Sum of variable costs constitutes the variable portion of total cost

## Formulas & Equations

- VC = f(Q)

## Examples

- Raw materials like electronic components for mobile phones
