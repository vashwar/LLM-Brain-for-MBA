# Volume Pricing

**Course:** Microeconomics
**Source:** Slides Week 3 - with notes.pdf

## Definition

A complex pricing strategy where the price per unit decreases as the number of units purchased increases, or where a flat fee allows for multiple units.

## Key Points

- Relevant when a single customer buys multiple units.
- Includes flat fees, bundling, and two-part tariffs.
- Potential risk of excessive use if marginal cost is positive.
