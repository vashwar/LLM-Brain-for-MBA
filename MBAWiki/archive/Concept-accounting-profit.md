# Accounting Profit

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Accounting profit is the difference between total revenue and explicit costs.  It's a measure of profitability used for financial reporting and tax purposes, but it doesn't consider opportunity costs.

## Key Points
- Total revenue minus explicit costs.
- Used for financial reporting and taxes.
- Does not consider implicit costs.
- Can differ significantly from economic profit.
- Accounting profit is useful to file your taxes or calculate the net worth of a firm
