# Comparative Statics

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Comparative statics is the analysis of how the market equilibrium changes in response to a change in underlying factors, such as supply or demand.  It involves comparing the initial equilibrium with the new equilibrium after the shock.

## Key Points
- Involves analyzing changes in market equilibrium.
- Examines the effects of shocks (e.g., changes in input costs, consumer preferences).
- Compares the initial equilibrium with the new equilibrium.
- Answers "what if" questions about the market.
