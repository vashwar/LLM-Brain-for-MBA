# Diminishing Marginal Utility

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
The principle of diminishing marginal utility states that as an individual consumes more of a good or service, the additional satisfaction (utility) derived from each additional unit decreases. At some point, the marginal utility can become zero or even negative.

## Key Points
- Marginal happiness that you get from each additional unit gets smaller and smaller.
- You get closer and closer to the point of satiation.
- Applies to all goods and services.
- Explains why demand curves are downward sloping.
- Related to Bill Gates' observation about hamburgers.
