# Economic Models

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Economic models are theoretical frameworks used to understand the world by stating assumptions and deriving conclusions through logical steps. They are simplifications of reality, often expressed mathematically or graphically.

## Key Points
- They are theoretical frameworks.
- Based on stated assumptions.
- Lead to conclusions through logical steps.
- Can be mathematical or graphical.
- Are always wrong, but sometimes useful.
