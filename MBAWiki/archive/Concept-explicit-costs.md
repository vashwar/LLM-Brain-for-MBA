# Explicit Costs

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Explicit costs are direct, out-of-pocket payments made by a firm for the use of its resources. These are easily quantifiable and typically involve a monetary transaction.

## Key Points
- Involve direct monetary payments.
- Easy to quantify.
- Include wages, rent, and raw material costs.
- Relevant for calculating accounting profit.
