# Homo Economicus

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Homo economicus is a theoretical construct of a perfectly rational, selfish, and informed economic agent used in economic modeling.  This concept simplifies decision-making processes for analytical purposes.

## Key Points
- 100% Selfish: They don't care how their actions will affect others.
- 100% Rational: Everyone is super smart and can make optimal decisions in a blink of an eye.
- 100% Informed: Everyone knows all the relevant information, including prices, wages, taxes, and so on.
- A simplification used in economic models.
- Not reflective of real-world human behavior.

## Step-by-Step Example
