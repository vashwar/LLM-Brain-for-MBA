# Implicit Costs

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Implicit costs represent the opportunity cost of using a firm's own resources, rather than selling or renting them to others. They do not involve direct monetary payments but represent foregone income.

## Key Points
- Do not involve direct monetary payments.
- Represent the opportunity cost of using resources.
- Include foregone salary or rent.
- Relevant for calculating economic profit.
- Accountants often do not count the implicit costs, but economists do and managers should.
