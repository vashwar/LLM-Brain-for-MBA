# Law of Demand

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
The Law of Demand states that, all other things being equal, as the price of a good or service increases, the quantity demanded decreases. This inverse relationship is fundamental to understanding demand curves.

## Key Points
- States an inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded.
- Underlies the downward-sloping demand curve.
- Assumes all other factors are constant (ceteris paribus).
- Reflects the willingness of consumers to pay less at higher prices.
- Related to the concept of diminishing marginal utility.
