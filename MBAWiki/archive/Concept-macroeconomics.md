# Macroeconomics

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Macroeconomics examines the overall performance of an economy, focusing on aggregated outcomes of individual choices. Key concerns include inflation, gross domestic product (GDP), and unemployment rates.

## Key Points
- Deals with aggregated individual choices.
- Focuses on the evolution of the average price (inflation rate).
- Concerned with the Gross Domestic Product (GDP).
- Studies economy-wide phenomena like unemployment and economic growth.
