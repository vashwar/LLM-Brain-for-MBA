# Market Equilibrium

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Market equilibrium occurs when the quantity of a good or service that suppliers are willing to supply equals the quantity that consumers are willing to demand. This point is characterized by an equilibrium price and quantity.

## Key Points
- Occurs where supply equals demand.
- Determines the equilibrium price and quantity.
- Represents a stable point in the market.
- Excess supply exists above the equilibrium price.
- Excess demand exists below the equilibrium price.
