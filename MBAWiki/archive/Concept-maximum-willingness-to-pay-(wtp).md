# Maximum Willingness to Pay (WTP)

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Maximum Willingness to Pay (WTP) represents the highest price a consumer is willing to pay for a product or service. It's a key determinant in shaping the demand curve.

## Key Points
- The maximum price a customer is willing to pay for a product.
- Used to determine the demand curve.
- Differs among consumers based on their valuation of the product.
- Related to consumer surplus calculation.
