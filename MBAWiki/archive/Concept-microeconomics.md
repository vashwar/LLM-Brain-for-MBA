# Microeconomics

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Microeconomics studies the individual decisions of firms and individuals, such as a consumer's choice to buy a product at a given price. It focuses on individual markets and the behavior of economic agents.

## Key Points
- Deals with individual decisions of firms and individuals.
- Focuses on specific markets.
- Examples include studying consumer choices and firm behavior.
- Contrasts with macroeconomics, which aggregates individual choices.
