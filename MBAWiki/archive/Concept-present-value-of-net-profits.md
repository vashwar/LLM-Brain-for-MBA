# Present Value of Net Profits

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
The present value of net profits represents the current worth of future profits, discounted to account for the time value of money.  It's used to evaluate the profitability of long-term investments and business decisions.

## Key Points
- The discounted value of future profits.
- Accounts for the time value of money.
- Used to evaluate long-term investments.
- A dollar today is better than a dollar tomorrow.
- The goal of the firm should be maximizing value
