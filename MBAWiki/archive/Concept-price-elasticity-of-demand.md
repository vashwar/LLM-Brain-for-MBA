# Price Elasticity of Demand

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Price elasticity of demand measures the responsiveness of the quantity demanded of a good or service to a change in its price. It indicates how much the quantity demanded will change for a given percentage change in price.

## Key Points
- Responsiveness of consumers to price changes.
- Ratio between two numbers.
- Calculus can be used to measure price elasticity.
- Can be used to find the profit-maximizing price for a product
- If the price goes up, will fewer people buy the goods?
