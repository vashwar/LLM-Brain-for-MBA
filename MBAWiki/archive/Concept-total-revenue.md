# Total Revenue

**Source:** Slides Week 1 Weekend - with notes.pdf

## Definition
Total revenue is the total income a firm receives from selling its goods or services. It is calculated by multiplying the price per unit by the quantity sold.

## Key Points
- Calculated as price multiplied by quantity.
- Represents the firm's income from sales.
- Can be depicted as the area of a rectangle on a demand curve graph.
- Used in profit calculations.

## Formulas & Equations
```
['TR=P*Q=(400-0.25Q)*Q=400Q-0.25Q2']
```

## Step-by-Step Example
If a pizza slice is sold for $3 and 4 slices are sold, the total revenue is $12.

## Real-World Applications
- Business planning
- Financial analysis
- Pricing strategy
